Authentic Borscht Recipe

Description

This borscht recipe comes from my friend's mother from Ukraine who taught me how to make this classic beet soup.
It's as authentic as it gets, it can be served vegetarian-style by omitting the sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pork sausage
  • 3 medium beets
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 medium baking potatoes
  • ½ medium head cabbage
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 8 ¾ cups water
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Crumble sausage into a skillet set over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until no longer pink. Remove from the heat and set aside.
  2. Fill a large pot halfway with water (about 8 cups) and bring to a boil.
  3. Add sausage to pot, cover pot, and return to a boil. Add beets and cook until they have lost their color.
    Add carrots and potatoes and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  4. Add cabbage and diced tomatoes to pot
  5. Add onion and cook until tender
  6. Add garlic to the soup, cover, and turn off the heat
  7. Ladle into serving bowls
